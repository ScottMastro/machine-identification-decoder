ColumnFieldDescription
1ParityRow parity column (odd parity per row)
2MinuteMinute the page was printed
3 – 4UnusedTypically zero
5HourHour the page was printed (24h)
6DayDay of month
7MonthMonth (1–12)
8YearYear without century (e.g. 26 = 2026)
9UnusedTypically zero
10SeparatorOften all 1s (127)
11 – 14SerialPrinter serial number, two decimal digits per column (most significant at col 14)
15Serial?Constant per printer, possibly part of serial number

Row 1 is a parity row. Each column's 7 data bits (rows 2–8) encode a value, MSB at row 2. All rows and columns maintain odd parity.
